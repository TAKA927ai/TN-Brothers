Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TN Brothers ("we," "us," or "our"), a global investment company headquartered in Hong Kong, collects, uses, and safeguards personal information provided by visitors and users of our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information when you interact with our website or submit inquiries:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, and any other details you voluntarily provide via our contact form.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on the site, and referring URLs, collected automatically through standard web technologies.
  • Communications: The content of messages or inquiries you send to us.
  • Technical Data: Device identifiers and operating system information used for website optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

TN Brothers uses collected information solely for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and providing requested information about our investment activities.
  • Maintaining and improving the functionality, security, and user experience of our website.
  • Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and regulatory requirements in Hong Kong and other jurisdictions.
  • Protecting the rights, property, and safety of TN Brothers and its stakeholders.
  • Conducting internal analytics to better understand how our website is used.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission.
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.
  • Regular review and updating of our data security practices.
  • Physical and electronic safeguards for our data processing systems.

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These may include:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Preference Cookies: Used to remember your language and display preferences.

You may configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that some features of our website may not function properly without cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We may utilize reputable third-party service providers to operate and improve our website, including web hosting, analytics, and contact form processing services. These providers are contractually bound to handle your information securely and only for the purposes specified by TN Brothers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites linked from our site.

6. International Data Transfers

TN Brothers operates from Hong Kong and may process personal data in jurisdictions outside your country of residence. By using our website, you consent to the transfer of your information to Hong Kong and any other country where we or our service providers operate, subject to appropriate safeguards consistent with applicable privacy laws.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access personal information we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • The right to object to or restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • The right to data portability where technically feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided in Section 9.

8. Retention of Data

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When personal information is no longer required, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: contact@tnbrothers.com
Response Time: Within 2 Business Days

10. Amendments to This Policy

TN Brothers reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed of how we protect your information. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
